# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

HeroForge is a security assessment tool designed exclusively for **authorized penetration testing, security research, and network administration**. We are committed to fostering a community that upholds the highest ethical standards in cybersecurity.

We pledge to make participation in our project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Ethical Use Standards

### Authorized Use Only

All community members must commit to using HeroForge **only** for:

- Security assessments with **explicit written authorization** from system owners
- Penetration testing engagements with proper contracts and scope definitions
- Educational purposes in controlled lab environments
- Personal network testing on systems you own
- Authorized bug bounty programs
- Defensive security research and network administration

### Prohibited Activities

The following are strictly prohibited and will result in immediate removal from the community:

- Using HeroForge on systems without explicit authorization
- Sharing techniques or knowledge intended to facilitate unauthorized access
- Discussing or promoting illegal activities
- Distributing malware, exploits for unauthorized use, or attack tools
- Targeting critical infrastructure, healthcare systems, or emergency services
- Any activity that violates applicable laws or regulations

## Community Standards

### Expected Behavior

- **Be ethical**: Always obtain proper authorization before security testing
- **Be respectful**: Treat all community members with dignity
- **Be constructive**: Provide helpful feedback and collaborate positively
- **Be responsible**: Report vulnerabilities through proper disclosure channels
- **Be educational**: Share knowledge to improve defensive security practices
- **Be professional**: Maintain high standards in all interactions

### Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind
- Trolling, insulting comments, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Discussing unauthorized access techniques or "black hat" activities
- Promoting or glorifying illegal hacking activities
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All project repositories and code contributions
- Issue trackers, pull requests, and code reviews
- Community discussions and forums
- Official project communication channels
- Representing the project at public events

## Enforcement

### Reporting

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project team at [conduct@heroforge.dev](mailto:conduct@heroforge.dev). All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

### Consequences

Community leaders will follow these guidelines in determining consequences:

1. **Warning**: Private written warning with clarity on the violation
2. **Temporary Ban**: Temporary removal from community interaction
3. **Permanent Ban**: Permanent removal from community participation

**Immediate permanent bans** will be issued for:
- Promoting or admitting to unauthorized system access
- Sharing tools or techniques specifically for malicious purposes
- Any illegal activity

## Legal Disclaimer

HeroForge is provided for **authorized security testing and educational purposes only**. Users are solely responsible for ensuring they have proper authorization before conducting any security assessments. Unauthorized access to computer systems is illegal in most jurisdictions and may result in civil and criminal penalties.

The HeroForge project, its maintainers, and contributors:
- Do not condone or support unauthorized access to any systems
- Are not responsible for misuse of this tool
- Actively discourage any illegal or unethical use
