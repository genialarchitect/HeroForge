### 5.3 Objection Handling

**Common VC Objections & Responses:**

**Q: "Why won't Tenable just add a customer portal and crush you?"**
> A: Tenable has 3,500 employees optimized for Fortune 500 sales. Their pricing ($2,275/yr) and feature bloat alienate SMB consultancies. We're attacking a segment they economically can't serve (Clayton Christensen's disruption playbook). By the time they react, we'll have 10,000+ consultancies locked in with multi-year contracts.

**Q: "What's your defensibility? Anyone can build a scanner."**
> A: Our moat is data + integrations. Each scan improves our ML models (5M+ vulnerabilities trained). Our customer portal has 3 years of UX refinement. Our compliance frameworks (PCI-DSS, HIPAA, SOC2) took 18 months to build. This isn't just a scanner—it's an ecosystem.

**Q: "How do you compete with free tools (OpenVAS, Nmap)?"**
> A: Free tools are for hobbyists. Professional consultancies need: (1) AI prioritization (not 10,000 false positives), (2) Client deliverables (branded reports, customer portal), (3) Compliance automation (SOC2 evidence collection). We sell time savings + client retention, not just scans.

**Q: "What if enterprises adopt continuous pentesting and cut out consultancies?"**
> A: 80% of enterprises lack in-house pentesting expertise (talent shortage). They'll always need consultancies for specialized tests (e.g., embedded systems, SCADA/ICS). We're empowering consultancies to deliver faster/cheaper, expanding the TAM, not replacing them.

**Q: "Why Series A now? Why not bootstrap longer?"**
> A: Three tailwinds converging: (1) AI in cybersecurity hitting $34B in 2025, (2) PTaaS models growing 21% CAGR, (3) SOC2 requirements exploding (GDPR, CCPA compliance). If we don't scale GTM now, we'll miss the window. Terra Security raised $30M in 4 months—speed matters.
