# HeroForge Refund Policy

**Last Updated:** December 24, 2024

## Overview

This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions for refunds on HeroForge subscriptions and services.

## Subscription Services

### General Policy

**HeroForge subscriptions are non-refundable.** When you subscribe to HeroForge, you are purchasing access to our security assessment platform for the subscription period.

### Why No Refunds?

1. **Immediate Access:** You receive immediate access to all features upon subscription
2. **Digital Service:** Our service is delivered electronically and cannot be "returned"
3. **Resource Allocation:** We allocate server resources upon subscription activation
4. **Scan Capabilities:** Scan credits and capabilities are immediately available

## Exceptions

We may consider refund requests in the following exceptional circumstances:

### Technical Issues
If HeroForge experiences a significant outage or technical issue that prevents you from using the Service for an extended period (more than 72 consecutive hours), you may be eligible for:
- Pro-rated credit for the affected period
- Subscription extension

### Billing Errors
If you were charged incorrectly due to our error:
- Duplicate charges will be refunded
- Incorrect amounts will be corrected

### Legal Requirements
Refunds may be provided where required by applicable consumer protection laws in your jurisdiction.

## Requesting a Refund

### How to Request
If you believe you qualify for an exception, contact us:
- Email: billing@heroforge.security
- Subject: "Refund Request - [Your Account Email]"
- Include: Reason for request, subscription details, any supporting documentation

### Response Time
We will respond to refund requests within 5 business days.

## Cancellation Policy

### How to Cancel
You may cancel your subscription at any time:
1. Go to Settings â†’ Subscription
2. Click "Cancel Subscription"
3. Confirm cancellation

### Effect of Cancellation
- Your subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period
- You will not be charged for the next billing period
- No refund is provided for unused time in the current period
- You retain access to your data for 30 days after expiration

### Data Export
Before cancellation, we recommend:
1. Exporting your scan data and reports
2. Downloading any necessary documentation
3. Saving your compliance records

## Free Trial

### Trial Period
If we offer a free trial:
- No payment is required during the trial
- You may cancel before the trial ends without charge
- If not cancelled, your subscription begins automatically

### Trial Limitations
- Trial access may have feature limitations
- Only one trial per user/organization

## Enterprise Agreements

Enterprise customers with custom contracts:
- Refund terms are as specified in your agreement
- Contact your account manager for refund requests

## Chargebacks

### Our Policy
If you initiate a chargeback with your bank or credit card company:
- Your account will be immediately suspended
- We will provide transaction documentation to dispute the chargeback
- Resolution may result in permanent account termination

### Before Disputing
Please contact us first at billing@heroforge.security. We are committed to resolving issues directly when possible.
